ABOUT US

Established in the year of 2020 during the COVID-19 pandemic. On You Cafe is a Bruneian Culture Food Cafe which aims to spread health awareness by producing healthy dishes with a twist of traditional Bruneian flavours. Our Signature Dish, the - 'Nasi Lemak Healthy' - was the first dish we launched, and it has received much positive feedback so far. There will more coming soon In Shaa Allah.

OBJECTIVE

"To reach the 2nd Maqasid Syariah (Objective of Syariah), The Preservation & Promotion of Life."

VISION

"To introduce a way of life following Rasullullah to the local community, reviving the dietary with local delicacy."

OUR STORY

The name "On You" came from an early childhood memory of the co-founders. While they were out drinking "Teh Tarik", they would point at each other, and say "On You ah~", the first who says it wins and the other person pays the bill. Over time, the phrase "On You" turned to a regular game. This game symbolizes the co-founders bond and friendship. So, "On You" is not just a catchy catch phrase, but it is the SYMBOL of their FRIENDSHIP.

NASI LEMAK HEALTHY

The idea of 'Nasi Lemak Sihat' came from two inspirations. The first was the 2nd Maqasid Syariah or Objective of the Syariah - The Preservation and Promotion of Life - which includes to maintain and to promote a healthy life. The second was the co-founders childhood friend who became a health consultant. He was diagnosed with a disease a few years ago, and went through diet programmes for years. Due to his experience, he was inspired to help others to reach the optimal level of a healthy lifestyle. Through many trials and failures, he eventually discovered the best ingredients for a tasty and healthy recipe, and that was how the idea of 'Nasi Lemak Healthy' materialised.
